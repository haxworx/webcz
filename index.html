Burnt Offering at God's Temple

Disclaimer

Burnt offerings are not a replacment for Jesus Christ's death on the cross!

This is an experiment in mathematics and Christian spirituality. This tool will create a response of random words from the Christian Bible using both hardware entropy and the data entered by the user. Essentially this means that your offering will affect the response given.

My suggestion is to enter a prayer of thanksgiving to God. Your data is used instantly in the computation and not stored.

It's very similar to a burnt offering that the Jewish people once made at the Temple for their sins. My hope is this "oracle" might provide some help to you.

This is an attempt at a holy software program. Your words will affect the result and possibly (probably) your spiritual intent will affect this too.

I think it's very important to use your intellect in ways to honour God in interesting and unique ways.

Think of new, interesting and exciting ways to praise God with your mind! Thank Him in all situations!
